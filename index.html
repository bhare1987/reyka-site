What is Rayka?

A travel app where friends share their favorite places to go, all around the world.

Explore

Tired of always asking friends for advice on where to go? Simply scroll through the feed to see the recommendations and favorite places of people you know and trust.

Share

Know of any great local places or travel spots your friends have to check out? Finally, a platform to share where you've been and what you recommend.

Discover

Tired of outdated rankings? Find just the best that the world has to offer from the favorites of your friends and of the public.

Master

Curious what the world's veteran travelers recommend? Find the best cities, bars, restaurants and more from experts who have travelled the globe.
